Neo-Classical

Neo-Classical was the same as classical.
Neo means new, but their were no new concepts in the Neo-Classical era. It was only a resurgence of the classical era.
Neo-Classical arcitecture consisted of a dome, a triangular pediment, colums, and of course symetry.
Neo classical arctecture is influenced by classical arcitecture more specificly greek and roman arcitecture. neo classical arcitecture also seems to hold a high stature. mainly for places of power. for example the white house in the U.S.A. Or the Bank of montreal in Canada
